Introduction

Welcome to our safety and security awareness campaign. Here, we emphasize the importance of creating a secure environment for everyone.

Tips for Personal Safety

  • Be aware of your surroundings.
  • Keep personal information confidential.
  • Use strong and unique passwords.
  • Report any suspicious activities to authorities.

Home Security

Ensure your home is secure by:

  • Installing quality locks on doors and windows.
  • Using timers for lights to give the appearance of someone being home.
  • Securing valuables in a safe place.
