SALAAR

Salaar is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action thriller film written and directed by Prashanth Neel, and produced by Vijay Kiragandur. It stars Prabhas as the titular character,[6] along with Prithviraj Sukumaran, Shruti Haasan, Jagapathi Babu, Tinnu Anand, Easwari Rao, Sriya Reddy, and Ramachandra Raju.The film's shoot began on 29 January 2021 in the coal mines near the city of Godavarikhani, Telangana.[24] First schedule was completed by February 2021.[25] Second schedule of the film began in August 2021 in Hyderabad.[26][27] About 30% of the shoot was completed by March 2022. Filming was halted due to the release of Prabhas' Radhe Shyam (2022) and Neel's K.G.F: Chapter 2 (2022) and was planned to be resumed in May.[28] However, Prabhas underwent a knee fracture in March during his vacation in Spain, which further delayed the shoot.[29][30] In June 2022, a new schedule began at Hyderabad's Ramoji Film City with Prabhas and Haasan joining the sets.[31] The team also shot a 20-minute long action sequence in the middle of the sea, spending about ₹10 crore on the scene.[32] Makers of Salaar reportedly employed Dark Centric Theme (DCT) technology to shoot the film, making it the first Indian film to do so. As a result the film's lighting pattern and color palette would be darker in color The film was announced in December 2020 with principal photography beginning in January 2021 near Godavarikhani, Telangana. The music is composed by Ravi Basrur, while the cinematography is done by Bhuvan Gowda.

GUNTUR KARAM

Guntur Kaaram (pronounced [ɡuntuːr kaːram]) (transl. Spice of Guntur) is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action drama film written and directed by Trivikram Srinivas, and produced by S. Radha Krishna under Haarika & Hassine Creations. It stars Mahesh Babu, Sreeleela, Meenakshi Chaudhary, Jagapathi Babu and Ramya Krishna. The film was officially announced in May 2021 under the tentative title SSMB28. Principal photography commenced on 12 September 2022 in Annapurna Studios, Hyderabad. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by Thaman S, with cinematography handled by Manoj Paramahamsa and editing handled by Naveen Nooli In August 2021, Pooja Hegde was reported to be the lead actress, marking her second collaboration with Mahesh Babu, after Maharshi (2019) and third collaboration with Trivikram, after Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava and Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo.[10] Samyuktha Menon and Anu Emmanuel were reported to play the second female lead,[11] before finalizing Sreeleela.[12] She would play Babu's uncle's daughter in the film.[12] In February 2023, Ramya Krishna is selected to play a key role in the film.[13] In March, Prakash Raj joined the cast to play Mahesh Babu's grandfather in the film.[14] Later Jayaram joined the cast.[15] In April, Jagapathi Babu was reported to play "a very endearing yet scary" character.[16] Mahesh Achanta was also confirmed as cast.[17] In June 2023, however, Hedge opted out of the film, owing to conflicting dates in her schedule. She was subsequently replaced by Meenakshi Chaudhary, who would star in the supporting role that was previously meant to be portrayed by Sreeleela. Sreeleela would, instead, star in Hegde’s lead role.[18] Rekha was reported to play Babu's mother in the film.[19] Sunil also plays an important role

SKANDA

Skanda (transl. Hindu God of War/Son of Lord Shiva; subtitled onscreen with The Attacker) is an upcoming Indian Telugu-language action film written and directed by Boyapati Sreenu and produced by Srinivasaa Chitturi under Srinivasaa Silver Screen in collaboration with Zee Studios. The film features Ram Pothineni, Sreeleela, Saiee Manjrekar and Prince Cecil.[1] The film was officially announced in February 2022, under the tentative title BoyapatiRAPO,[2] while the official title Skanda was announced in July 2023.[3] Principal photography began in October 2022,[4] and wrapped in August 2023.[5] The music is composed by Thaman S, while the cinematography and editing are handled by Santosh Detake and Tammiraju, respectively. Skanda is scheduled to be released on 15 September 2023 Skanda is scheduled to release on 15 September 2023 in Telugu along with dubbed versions of Hindi, Tamil, Kannada and Malayalam languages.[17] It was initially scheduled for a release on 20 October 2023, however, to avoid a clash with Tiger Nageswara Rao, Leo and Ghost, it was preponed to 15 September 2023.
